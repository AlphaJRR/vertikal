# COMPANY OPERATING RHYTHM — VERTIKAL, LLC.

**Effective:** January 1, 2025  
**Status:** ✅ Locked

---

## WEEKLY RHYTHM

### CTO Weekly (Mondays, 9 AM)
**Duration:** 30 minutes  
**Attendees:** CTO, Founder (optional)

**Agenda:**
1. **Stability Check** (10 min)
   - App crashes / errors
   - Performance issues
   - Infrastructure health
   - Security incidents

2. **Release Status** (10 min)
   - Current sprint progress
   - Blockers and risks
   - Next release plan
   - Deployment schedule

3. **Quick Wins** (10 min)
   - Quick fixes completed
   - Optimizations made
   - Team updates

**Output:** Weekly tech status update (email)

---

### CMO Weekly (Tuesdays, 10 AM)
**Duration:** 30 minutes  
**Attendees:** CMO, Founder (optional)

**Agenda:**
1. **Narrative Check** (10 min)
   - Messaging clarity
   - Brand consistency
   - Content performance
   - Social media metrics

2. **Traction Review** (10 min)
   - Creator pipeline
   - Investor pipeline
   - Network pipeline
   - Conversion metrics

3. **Quick Wins** (10 min)
   - Content published
   - Partnerships initiated
   - Press mentions
   - Team updates

**Output:** Weekly marketing status update (email)

## MONTHLY RHYTHM

### Board Packet (Last Friday of Month)
**Duration:** 2 hours (prep)  
**Owner:** Founder (with team input)

**Sections:**
1. Executive Summary
2. Product Status
3. Technology
4. Brand & Market
5. Growth & Pipeline
6. Risks & Mitigation
7. Next 30 / 60 / 90 Days

**Output:** Board Packet PDF

**Distribution:**
- Board members
- Advisors
- Key executives

---

### Metrics Review (First Monday of Month)
**Duration:** 1 hour  
**Attendees:** Full team

**Agenda:**
1. **Key Metrics** (20 min)
   - App stability score
   - Web readiness score
   - Creator pipeline
   - Investor pipeline
   - User metrics (when available)

2. **Trends** (20 min)
   - Month-over-month changes
   - Growth trajectories
   - Risk indicators

3. **Action Items** (20 min)
   - What's working
   - What's not working
   - What needs attention

**Output:** Monthly metrics dashboard

---

### Risk Scan (Second Monday of Month)
**Duration:** 30 minutes  
**Attendees:** Leadership team

**Agenda:**
1. **Technical Risks** (10 min)
   - Infrastructure
   - Security
   - Performance

2. **Market Risks** (10 min)
   - Competition
   - Market changes
   - User adoption

3. **Operational Risks** (10 min)
   - Team
   - Funding
   - Execution

**Output:** Risk register update

---

## QUARTERLY RHYTHM

### Strategy Reset (First Week of Quarter)
**Duration:** Half day  
**Attendees:** Full team

**Agenda:**
1. **Review Past Quarter** (1 hour)
   - What worked
   - What didn't work
   - Key learnings

2. **Set Quarter Goals** (1 hour)
   - Product goals
   - Growth goals
   - Team goals

3. **Plan Quarter** (2 hours)
   - Roadmap alignment
   - Resource allocation
   - Milestone planning

**Output:** Quarterly strategy document

---

### Roadmap Realignment (Second Week of Quarter)
**Duration:** 2 hours  
**Attendees:** Product, Engineering, Design

**Agenda:**
1. **Review Roadmap** (30 min)
   - Current state
   - Changes needed
   - Dependencies

2. **Prioritize Features** (1 hour)
   - User value
   - Business value
   - Technical feasibility

3. **Plan Sprint** (30 min)
   - Next sprint goals
   - Resource needs
   - Timeline

**Output:** Updated roadmap

---

### Capital Planning (Third Week of Quarter)
**Duration:** 2 hours  
**Attendees:** Founder, CFO (when hired)

**Agenda:**
1. **Review Finances** (30 min)
   - Current burn rate
   - Runway
   - Budget vs actual

2. **Plan Next Quarter** (1 hour)
   - Budget allocation
   - Hiring plan
   - Investment needs

3. **Fundraising Status** (30 min)
   - Current pipeline
   - Next steps
   - Timeline

**Output:** Capital plan

---

## COMMUNICATION STANDARDS

### Email
- **Response Time:** 24 hours (business days)
- **Format:** Clear subject, concise body, action items
- **CC:** Only when necessary

### Slack
- **Channels:** #general, #product, #engineering, #marketing
- **Response Time:** 4 hours (business hours)
- **Format:** Clear messages, @mentions for action

### Meetings
- **Default:** 30 minutes
- **Agenda:** Required (sent 24h before)
- **Notes:** Required (sent 24h after)
- **Video:** Required (remote)
