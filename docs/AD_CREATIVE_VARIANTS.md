## V1: THE ANTI-ALGORITHM MANIFESTO

### Creative Concept
**Format:** Text-on-screen video with dramatic music  
**Duration:** 15-30 seconds  
**Style:** Minimalist, high-impact text overlays

### Visual Elements
- Black background
- Gold text (#FFD700)
- Bold, sans-serif font
- Text animates in sync with music
- No talking head, pure text

### Copy Structure

**Hook (0-3s):**
```
"Stop Making 60-Second Sacrifices."
```

**Problem (3-10s):**
```
"You spend months creating a series.
You post it.
You get... nothing.
No reach. No money. Just 'likes'."
```

**Solution (10-20s):**
```
"We built Vertikal for creators
who refuse to wait for permission.
Series. Docs. Reality.
Get paid from day one."
```

**CTA (20-30s):**
```
"Apply for Founding 50"
[Button: "Apply Now"]
```

### Music
- Dramatic, cinematic score
- Builds tension, resolves on CTA
- No lyrics, instrumental only

### Target Audience
- **Primary:** Creators frustrated with algorithm
- **Interest:** Content creation, video production
- **Behavior:** Posts long-form content, low engagement

### Goal
**Highest Volume Prospecting**  
**Expected CPC:** $1.50-$2.00
