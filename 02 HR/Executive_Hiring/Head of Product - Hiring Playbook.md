# HEAD OF PRODUCT — HIRING PLAYBOOK

**Role:** Head of Product  
**Priority:** High (Next highest leverage after CTO + CMO)  
**Status:** Ready to Hire (Post-Funding)

---

## WHY THIS ROLE

### The Gap
- **Vision → Features:** Need translation layer
- **Ambiguity:** Kill feature creep and scope drift
- **Roadmap Truth:** Single source of truth for product decisions
- **User Truth:** Enforce user-first thinking

### The Impact
- **Velocity:** Faster feature delivery
- **Quality:** Better product decisions
- **Alignment:** Cross-functional clarity
- **User Focus:** Data-driven prioritization

---

## RESPONSIBILITIES

### Core Functions

1. **Product Strategy**
   - Translate vision into product roadmap
   - Define feature priorities
   - Balance user needs with business goals
   - Own product-market fit metrics

2. **Feature Specification**
   - Write clear product specs
   - Define acceptance criteria
   - Collaborate with engineering
   - Ensure quality standards

3. **Prioritization**
   - Build and maintain backlog
   - Make trade-off decisions
   - Balance short-term vs long-term
   - Data-driven prioritization

4. **Cross-Functional Alignment**
   - Align engineering, design, marketing
   - Communicate roadmap changes
   - Manage stakeholder expectations
   - Bridge vision and execution

5. **User Truth Enforcement**
   - User research and insights
   - Data analysis and metrics
   - User feedback synthesis
   - Advocate for user needs

## INTERVIEW PROCESS

### Round 1: Screen (30 min)
- **Who:** Founder / CTO
- **Focus:** Experience, cultural fit, motivation
- **Questions:**
  - Why Vertikal?
  - What's your product philosophy?
  - How do you prioritize features?
  - Tell me about a product decision you made

### Round 2: Product Case Study (60 min)
- **Who:** Founder / CTO / CMO
- **Focus:** Product thinking, problem-solving
- **Exercise:** Product case study (TBD)
- **Questions:**
  - How would you prioritize features?
  - How do you balance user needs vs business goals?
  - How do you measure product success?

### Round 3: Technical Deep Dive (45 min)
- **Who:** CTO
- **Focus:** Technical understanding, collaboration
- **Questions:**
  - How do you work with engineering?
  - How do you handle technical constraints?
  - How do you communicate technical requirements?

### Round 4: Culture Fit (30 min)
- **Who:** Founder / Team
- **Focus:** Mission alignment, cultural fit
- **Questions:**
  - Why vertical cinema?
  - Why Black cultural ownership?
  - How do you handle ambiguity?
  - What's your working style?

### Round 5: Reference Checks
- **Who:** Founder
- **Focus:** Performance, collaboration, impact
- **References:** 3 (2 former managers, 1 peer)
