VIBECODE

VibeCode is Vertikal's real-time comment overlay system. Comments flow across the screen as you watch, creating a shared viewing experience without interrupting the content.

Real-Time Emotional Reactions

Comments appear in real-time as viewers react to moments. No scrolling, no pausing. Pure immersion.

Replacement of Low-Signal Comments

VibeCode replaces traditional comment sections with contextual, real-time engagement. No noise, just signal.

Engagement Signals & Heatmaps

Heatmaps show where viewers engage most. Creators see what resonates. Data-driven storytelling.

Algorithm-Free

No engagement bait. No forced virality. VibeCode amplifies authentic reactions, not manufactured metrics.
