HOW THE ENGINE WORKS.

Vertikal is not just a feed. It is a closed-loop economy for cinematic IP.

STEP 01

The Cinematic Feed

No dance trends. No noise. A curated, noise-free stream of 2–10 minute serialized dramas, thrillers, and comedies. Vertical cinema, optimized for retention.

STEP 02

VIBE™ Layer

This is where the culture lives. Viewers react to specific timestamps with memes, audio clips, and text. It creates a "live stadium" feel on every video.

STEP 03

The Labor Market

See a show you love? Click the creator's profile. See open roles for their next season. Apply directly with your Vertikal profile. We turn viewers into crews.

STEP 04

Ownership & Monetization

Creators own their IP. The V Badge system unlocks direct transaction fees, ad-revenue sharing, and licensing opportunities without the middleman.
