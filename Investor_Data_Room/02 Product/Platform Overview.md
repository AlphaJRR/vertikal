## CORE FEATURES

### 1. Vertical Cinema Originals
**Description:** Premium short-form vertical series, long-form, and documentary content built for the 2-10 minute cinematic window.

**Key Features:**
- 9:16 aspect ratio optimized
- High production value
- Franchise-building focus
- IP ownership retained

**Status:** ✅ Production Ready

---

### 2. VIBE™ (Live Overlay Audience Layer)
**Description:** Real-time comments flow over content, creating shared viewing experiences without interruption.

**Key Features:**
- Danmaku-style overlay
- Real-time synchronization
- User-controlled (on/off)
- Engagement amplification

**Status:** ✅ Implemented (disabled on mount for stability)

**Technical Details:**
- Delayed initialization (1s delay)
- User-controlled enable/disable
- Performance optimized

---

### 3. V Badge System
**Description:** Trust + access + monetization paths. Signals contribution, priority, and ownership. Earned, not bought.

**Badge Types:**
- **Gold:** Founding 50 (capped at 50 creators)
- **Green:** Investors (capital partners)
- **Blue:** Builders (engineers & operators)
- **Titanium:** Networks (studios & infrastructure)

**Status:** ✅ Implemented and documented

---

### 4. Jobs Inside Platform
**Description:** Every creator profile becomes a hiring hub. Post cast and crew roles directly from productions.

**Key Features:**
- Integrated job posting
- Applicant management
- No external job boards
- Hiring where work lives

**Status:** ✅ UI Complete, Backend Integration Pending

---

### 5. IP Ownership
**Description:** Creators retain ownership of content and audience. Revenue flows back to creators.

**Key Features:**
- Content ownership retained
- Audience ownership retained
- Revenue sharing model
- Franchise IP protection

**Status:** ✅ Legal framework in place
